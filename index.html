about me

I am a passionate 5th year mechanical engineering student @ ubc specializing in mechatronics with a desire to improve the lives of others through engineering. I love applying my multidisciplinary knowledge to design and prototyping products. My interests lie in mechanical and product design, robotics, manufacturing and implementing the integration of mechanical, electrical, and software into my designs.

I am currently interning at Tesla as a mechanical hardware design engineer, and have previously worked at TRIUMF as a mechanical design engineer, Ability Healthcare in product design and manufacturing, and the UBC MEMS Laboratory as a soft robotics researcher.
